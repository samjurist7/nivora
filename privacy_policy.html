Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

ShishaX ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ShishaX mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

This App is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address – used for registration, login, account activation, and password reset.
  • Display name – the name you choose to show inside the App.
  • Password – stored as a one-way cryptographic hash (bcrypt); we never store your plain-text password.

Device & Usage Data

  • Bluetooth device identifiers and names when you pair a ShishaX device.
  • Device control commands and logs sent through the cloud or Bluetooth.
  • Screensaver and preset images you choose to upload to the cloud.

Technical Data

  • Authentication tokens (JWT) stored temporarily on your device via SharedPreferences.
  • Server-side logs (IP address, request timestamps) for security and debugging.

We do not collect location data, payment data, contacts, or any biometric information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate you and keep your session secure.
  • Enable remote control of your paired ShishaX device via Bluetooth or cloud.
  • Send transactional emails (account activation, password reset). No marketing emails.
  • Monitor server health and diagnose technical issues.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers.

We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: Our email delivery service (QQ Mail / SMTP) receives your email address solely to send transactional messages.
  • Legal obligations: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights of our users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Device logs are retained for up to 90 days. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

5. Data Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage. Authentication uses JWT tokens with expiry limits. We use industry-standard practices to protect data in transit and at rest. However, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password.

6. Bluetooth Usage

The App uses Bluetooth to communicate directly with your ShishaX device. Bluetooth data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use a cloud-control feature.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this may affect your ability to use the App).
